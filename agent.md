# GadgetGrid - Project Workflow & Master Plan

## 🎨 Unique Light Theme Selections
Since you want something unique and professional (avoiding the common blue/green combinations), here are two very modern, premium Light Theme palettes. We will configure Tailwind with one of these:

### Theme 1: "Copper & Slate" (Earthy & Premium Tech)
*Highly professional, gives a very premium and trustworthy vibe.*
- **Background:** Pearl White (`#FAFAFA`)
- **Primary Accent:** Copper/Rust (`#D97757`) - *Used for primary buttons, CTA, active links*
- **Secondary Accent:** Slate Teal (`#2C5282`) - *Used for icons, secondary buttons, badges*
- **Text:** Dark Charcoal (`#1A202C`)

### Theme 2: "Sunset Minimalist" (Warm, Trendy & Inviting)
*Very trendy among modern startups, looks clean and energetic.*
- **Background:** Soft Ivory (`#FEFCFB`)
- **Primary Accent:** Warm Coral (`#F08A5D`) - *Outstanding visibility for buttons*
- **Secondary Accent:** Deep Navy (`#183153`) - *For contrast in footers and headers*
- **Text:** Slate Gray (`#334155`)
